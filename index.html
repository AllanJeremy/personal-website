ABOUT ME

I love solving problems

In 2009, I joined high school. A young, ambitious student curious about solving problems. I knew I wanted to create solutions to make people's lives easier but I didn't know how. It wasn't long before my Computer studies teacher noticed I had a burning desire to learn, afterwhich he took me under his wing, so to speak. Through him, I learnt the basics of writing pseudocodes, algorithms as well as how programs worked in that year (I was 14-15 years old at this point). He also taught me the basics of visual basic. That was the beginning of my journey as a developer.

A full story of how I got to where I am now would warrant for a longer string of text, and therefore I shall not go into too much detail from here on. Fast forward to 2017, I can now create the solutions I always wanted to create and I enjoy doing it. I also make YouTube videos in my spare time to share what I have learnt.
